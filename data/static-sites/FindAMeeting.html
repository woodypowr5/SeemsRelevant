GET TO KNOW US

Learn what a difference People to People travel can make in your life - straight from the source! There are two ways for you to hear first-person perspectives from travelers, parents, and teacher leaders about their global experiences with us.

IN-PERSON LOCAL MEETINGS

These meetings are hosted in your area, featuring everything you need to know about student travel. It's the perfect time to meet the teachers who will be leading your local trips and talk with students and parents who've already traveled.

ONLINE MEETINGS

Anytime, anywhere, you can tune in to an online meeting to discover what makes People to People travel so unique. (And, this is a great way to share the trips with family and friends!)

LET THE WORLD BE YOUR CLASSROOM

It’s like no classroom you’ve ever been in… and no field trip you’ve ever taken

Travel with People to People opens the world to you and your students. After a trip filled with hands-on learning, cross-cultural encounters, and leadership-building activities, you’re ready to teach at a whole new level. And your students are ready for their future in our highly connected world.

Learn more about what makes People to People unique, our strong support structure for leaders, and what’s in it for you as an educator.

BECOME A LEADER

Travel with a People to People group enhances your life as an educator. Give your traveling students experiences and lessons they’ll remember for the rest of their lives. At the same time, you’ll absorb facts, flavors, and cultures that bring new global insights to your students back home.

PICK YOUR PROGRAM

There are three ways teachers can lead People to People travelers in grades 5-12. It all depends on where you want to go and what you want to learn with your students!

Lead a regional or national group

You can focus on teaching while we fill your group roster. Students choose unique trips to destinations in the U.S. and around the world, and you typically lead students who are all from your area so it’s easy to prepare and travel together.

Travel your classroom

Build and lead your own travel group. You pick your destination, while we provide you with support to enroll your students and take care of every detail of your trip.

Facilitate a student leadership program

Work with a group of high-achieving students from across the country and the world who participate on our student leadership programs in Washington, D.C., and prestigious colleges throughout the U.S.

LEADER REQUIREMENTS

  • A minimum of 2 years experience leading a classroom or student group
  • A passion for growing students' 21st-century skills
  • Sufficient stamina for program activities, including walking more than six miles a day, hiking uneven terrain, and supervising students

STEPS TO BECOMING A LEADER

  1. Find the program that is best for you
  2. Create a login on our leader portal, the Compass
  3. Complete the leader application of the Compass Site
  4. Each applicant goes through a personal interview and background check.
